\subsection{Simulation parameters}

A number of parameters depend on the amount of particles per meter
(\cppid{ppm}).

The particle mass $m$ depends on the density $\rho$ and the amount of particles
per meter (\cppid{ppm}).

\[
m = \rho \cdot ppm^3
\]

Another relevant parameter is the \textmark{smoothing length} ($h$), which
depends on the \textmark{radius multiplier} ($r$) and amount of particles per
meter.

\[
h = \frac{r}{ppm}
\]
